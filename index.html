A Gross Poem

August 18, 2022

i think all of my loves have been tinged with a fog of melancholy
except for you
you are joy
and light
and soft whimpers in the dark
you are comfort
and warmth
and sunlight over your skin
you are familiar
and strange
and an "i've been here before"
you are passion
and ecstasy
and "please. please. please."
and i am so scared
and delighted
and i will always beg you for more
